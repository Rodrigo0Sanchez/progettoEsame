What is PetBase?

Dogs must also be cared for by being fed, watered, washed and brushed.
Once a day, they also need to be taken outside so that they can do their business and perhaps meet other dogs.
Fortunately, in PetBase the sweet creatures cannot die, but if neglected they will not fail to make their displeasure known to you.

 

With this application you can:

- Store all your dog's personal data, such as name, weight, date of birth, chip.
- Keep track of all visits made and future visits.
- Keep track of all vaccinations and booster shots.
- Save reminders of administrations/visits
- Save all administrations made or to be made.
- Search for and contact your nearest vet
- Manage your vets
- Manage multiple dogs
- Backup/Restore
